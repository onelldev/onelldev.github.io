TallyTV Support

Need help with TallyTV? Whether you're having trouble syncing with Simkl or Trakt, or you have a question about your "Remove Ads" purchase, we're here to help.

Common Troubleshooting

  • Sync Issues: Ensure you have an active internet connection and that you are logged into your Trakt/Simkl account in the app settings.
  • Purchases: If your premium features aren't appearing, try the "Restore Purchases" button in the Settings menu.
