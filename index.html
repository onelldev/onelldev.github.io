Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

TallyTV ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any contact information.
  • Media Syncing: TallyTV stores Simkl and Trakt authentication tokens locally on your device. We never see your passwords.
  • In-App Purchases: Handled by Apple; we receive only a verification token to unlock features.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services which may collect data:

  • Google AdMob: For serving ads (may use device Advertising IDs).
  • Simkl / Trakt: For media tracking as requested by the user.

4. Data Retention

All app settings and sync data are stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.
