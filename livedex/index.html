LiveDex Support

Help and contact information for LiveDex - Lookup Guide, a fast lookup companion for scanning and searching Pokemon details.

Contact Support

If you need help with LiveDex, found incorrect information, or want to report a bug, email support and include your device model, OS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

onellromandev@gmail.com

Common Help Topics

  • Camera scanning: make sure camera permission is enabled for LiveDex in your device settings.
  • Pokemon lookup: use the camera scanner or search by name to view evolution requirements, spawn locations, learnable moves, shiny forms, and type information.
  • Missing or unexpected data: send the Pokemon name and the detail that looks incorrect so it can be reviewed.

Privacy

LiveDex uses camera access only for scanning and lookup features. Camera access can be disabled at any time in your device settings.
